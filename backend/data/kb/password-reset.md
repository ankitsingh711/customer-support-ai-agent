# How do I reset my password?

## Standard password reset

Go to the login page and click **Forgot password**. Enter the email address on your
account and we'll send a reset link that is valid for 30 minutes. Click the link and
choose a new password of at least 8 characters, including one number.

## I didn't receive the reset email

Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there after 5 minutes, confirm you're
using the email address associated with your account (not a secondary email), then
request the reset again. If it still doesn't arrive, contact support to verify your
account email on file.

## I no longer have access to the email on my account

For security, we cannot reset a password without verifying account ownership. Contact
support with your full name, account creation date (approximate is fine), and the last
4 digits of any payment method on file so an agent can manually verify your identity.
This case always requires a human agent — never send a reset link for a changed email
address automatically.
