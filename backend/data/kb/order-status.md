# Where's my order?

## Order tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking link.
You can also check order status anytime by logging into your account and going to
**Account > Orders**. Standard orders arrive in 3-5 business days; expedited orders arrive
in 1-2 business days.

## My order hasn't arrived and it's past the estimated delivery date

If your order is more than 2 business days past its estimated delivery date, contact
support with your order number and we'll investigate with the carrier. Do not place a
duplicate order — refunds or reshipments for lost packages are handled case-by-case
once the carrier investigation completes (usually 3-5 business days).

## Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

We can update the shipping address only if the order has not yet entered the "processing"
stage. Once an order shows "processing" or later, the address is locked and cannot be
changed; you'll need to contact the carrier directly or arrange a return once delivered.
