# What's your refund policy?

## Standard refund window

Items can be returned for a full refund within 30 days of delivery, provided they are
unused and in original packaging. Refunds are issued to the original payment method
and typically post within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

## Digital products and subscriptions

Digital products are refundable within 14 days of purchase only if not yet downloaded
or activated. Subscription charges are refundable within 7 days of the billing date if
the subscription has not been used during that period; usage is determined by login
activity, not intent.

## Damaged or defective items

Damaged or defective items can be returned at any time within 90 days of delivery for a
full refund or replacement, no restocking fee applies. Please include photos of the
damage when contacting support to speed up processing.

## Non-refundable situations

Custom or personalized items, gift cards, and items marked "final sale" at checkout are
not eligible for refund. If a customer disputes this after a chargeback has already been
filed with their bank, escalate to a human agent — do not attempt to resolve chargebacks
automatically.
